The pandas project¶

pandas is an open source, BSD-licensed library providing high-performance, easy-to-use data structures and data analysis tools for the Python programming language.

Community¶

pandas actively supported today by a community of like-minded individuals around the world who contribute their valuable time and energy to help make open source pandas possible. Thank you to all of our contributors.

pandas is a NUMFocus sponsored project. This will help ensure the success of development of pandas as a world-class open-source project.
